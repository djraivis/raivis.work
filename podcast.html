Let's Share Something Interesting!!

This is a language exchange music podcast where I’m challenging myself to speak and interview Japanese people in Japanese. It’s called “Let’s Share Something Interesting” orientated for electronic dance music. Originally deployed on Soundcloud from where every new episode automatically get synchronized and published to all other platforms which are Spotify, Apple Music and dedicated website of the podcast: https://jpn.netlify.app
