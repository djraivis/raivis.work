I was lucky to make a Japanese friend with whom I can do a language exchange (English-Japanese). We sent each other voice messages to correct our pronunciation and this is how eventually we got an idea to start our podcast. Originally deployed on Soundcloud from where every new episode gets automatically published to all other platforms which are Spotify; Apple Music and our website of the podcast: https://jpn.netlify.app
